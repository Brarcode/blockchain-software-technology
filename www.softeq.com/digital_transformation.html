Digital Transformation Services

As experienced digital transformation service providers, we help you understand emerging technologies, manage ideation, build business cases, and deliver disruptive solutions. We don’t just offer innovation in a slide presentation; we roll up our sleeves and build together. We help you develop new models; enhance devices, equipment, and non-electronic objects with sensors; implement smart controls; and tap into sensor data to become more efficient and reach new markets.

Get in Touch for Details

Connected Products and Services

Get in Touch

We convert devices into a source of data to help consumer electronics brands explore additional revenue models. You can count on our technology transformation services to:

  • Prototype, design, and test a customer-facing gadget
  • Enhance smart devices with new features
  • Create web, mobile, and embedded applications for a connected product
  • Track and process device usage data to personalize customer experience

Asset Management

Get in Touch

We design systems that keep track of industrial assets, goods, and materials. The solutions enable manufacturing, automotive, logistics, and retail companies to:

  • Monitor assets using sensors and RFID tags
  • Facilitate picking and packing operations in the warehouse
  • Streamline inventories and automate replenishment
  • Optimize fuel consumption and route planning

Preventive & Predictive Maintenance

Get in Touch

We add sensors to industrial equipment and apply Machine Learning to process the data they produce so that our customers can:

  • Monitor equipment condition and health status
  • Anticipate failure
  • Boost customer service
  • Optimize manufacturing operations
  • Fine-tune machinery performance (onsite and remotely)

A Digital Transformation Company

Consulting

As a digital transformation consulting provider, we collaborate with the stakeholders in your company to define your project objectives, outline the project scope, create the high-level architecture of your cyber-physical system, and assess development costs.

Strategy

We begin with business analysis to rule out technology barriers. Our digital transformation service team then creates an iterative project roadmap to deliver business benefits (a technical vision, prototype, features) with each phase.

Implementation

As a digital transformation company, we follow software and hardware development best practices, including DevOps, to create a scalable, secure product. To keep it up-to-date after the warranty period, Softeq provides comprehensive customer support as part of our IT transformation services offer.
